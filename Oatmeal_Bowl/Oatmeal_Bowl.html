Simple and Delicious Oatmeal Recipe

A practical recipe for an energetic start to the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1.5 cups milk or water
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • Half a teaspoon of cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Topping: Blueberries, banana, strawberries, nuts, etc.

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, milk or water in a small saucepan.
  2. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly for 5-7 minutes. Remove from heat when the consistency thickens.
  3. Stir in honey, cinnamon, and salt.
  4. Pour into a bowl and top with your favorite fruits or nuts before serving.
